Case Studies

Jesse, 46

I live in Los Angeles, USA.


I work at a newspaper. I write about culture and social issues.


I think that culture is one of the most beneficial way of addressing social issues.

We have a video team but it is always busy producing content for our website.

I would like to share a detailed analysis about the prison system in California.

The title is Outside Man


    The key issues of my article are:

  • Scott Budnick produced the Hangover movies
  • But he is also strongly committed to the California prison reform
  • He believes in alternative education programs for young jailed people.

Leight, 28

I live in Asheville, USA.


I am a freelance journalist. I write about science.


I strongly believe that understanding science improves our everyday life.

I envy my colleagues that are able to produce video reports. It seems that their content has much more visibility and audience response.

I’ve written a critical story about the involvement of the US government in the polio vaccination in Pakistan.

The title is How is Polio Still a Thing?


    The key issues of my article are:

  • Why Pakistan is the center of Polio’s comeback
  • It would take just two months to completely eradicate it
  • How US and CIA are involved in the Polio comeback.

Stefano, 40

I live in Rome, Italy.


I write for a weekly magazine. I work on reportages.


I think multi-media reportages are the best combination of words, pictures and videos.

Our newsroom is really small and we feel the lack of an in-house video team that completes our news offer.

I am working on a reportage about the condition of war refugees that are trying to come to Europe via see.

The title is Seimila euro per essere abbandonati in mezzo al mare


    The key issues of my article are:

  • This kind of journey is really expensive and highly dangerous
  • Most of the people are from the middle class
  • They want to go to Northern Europe
  • Turkey is the new departure port of refugees.

Graeme, 34

I live in Washington, USA.


I work for a liberal newspaper. I write about world news, especially from the Middle East and the Arab world.


If you want to understand what terrorism, is you must talk and discuss with terrorists

I have never had the chance to learn how to make a video but I know it would be helpful to engage with younger generations.

I am going to release a long-form article about the intentions of the Islamic State.

The title is What ISIS Really Wants


    The key issues of my article are:

  • The Islamic State is no mere collection of psychopaths
  • The reality is that the Islamic State is very Islamic
  • They want to build a complete society following the example of Muhammad.
